Welcome to Happy Home Shop

We are a great team with the mission to bring out the best products to make your home the best place.

Our Mission

At Happy Home, our mission is to inspire and elevate the everyday living experience by offering a curated selection of high-quality accessories for the bedroom, bathroom, and home decoration. We are dedicated to bringing comfort, style, and joy into every corner of your home. Our commitment to exceptional design, sustainable practices, and unparalleled customer service ensures that each product not only enhances your living space but also aligns with your values and aspirations. At Happy Home, we believe that a beautiful home fosters a happy life, and we are here to make that vision a reality for you.

Our History

2010: The Beginning

Happy Home was founded in 2010 by Emily and David Thompson, a passionate duo with a shared vision of creating beautiful, functional, and affordable home accessories. The inspiration for the company came from their own experiences of transforming their first home into a sanctuary of comfort and style. They realized that while there were many options available, finding high-quality, thoughtfully designed home accessories that didn’t break the bank was a challenge.

2010-2012: Early Growth

Starting as a small online store, Happy Home initially focused on bedroom and bathroom accessories, offering a carefully selected range of products that combined both practicality and aesthetics. The Thompsons were committed to sourcing materials responsibly and ensuring that each item met their high standards for quality and design. Their dedication quickly resonated with customers, and the business began to grow.

2013: Expansion of Product Line

By 2013, Happy Home had expanded its product line to include a broader array of home decoration items. This included everything from stylish throw pillows and cozy blankets to elegant vases and wall art. The company also introduced seasonal collections, allowing customers to refresh their homes throughout the year with new and exciting pieces.

2015: Opening of First Brick-and-Mortal Store

In 2015, Happy Home opened its first brick-and-mortar store in their hometown, offering customers a chance to experience their products firsthand. The store became a community favorite, known for its warm and inviting atmosphere, much like the homes it helped to adorn. This success led to the opening of several more locations across the country over the next few years.

2016 - present

Throughout its growth, Happy Home has remained committed to its founding principles of quality, affordability, and customer satisfaction. The company has also embraced sustainability, implementing eco-friendly practices and offering a range of products made from recycled and natural materials. In 2018, Happy Home launched an initiative to partner with local artisans, showcasing unique, handcrafted items that add a personal touch to any space. Today, Happy Home continues to thrive, with a strong online presence and numerous retail locations. Emily and David's original vision lives on, as Happy Home remains dedicated to helping people create spaces that are not only beautiful but also a true reflection of their personal style and values. As they look to the future, the company aims to further expand its product lines and sustainability efforts, always with the goal of making every home a happy home.
